Who we are

Sanolyn Health is a health‑tech and wellness innovation company based in Nairobi, Kenya focused on patient-centered solutions that combine clinical nutrition, chronic disease management and digital health systems.

Vision

To be a leader in delivering integrated, innovative, and sustainable health and nutrition solutions in Africa.

Mission

To empower individuals and communities to achieve optimal health through access to quality care, functional nutrition, and digital innovations.

Problems we solve

  • High burden of NCDs (diabetes, hypertension, obesity)
  • Limited access to specialized nutrition services
  • Poor medication adherence and last-mile delivery gaps
  • Fragmented manual workflows across care teams
